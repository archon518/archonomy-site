Foundation

What ARCHONOMY Is

ARCHONOMY is the discipline that restores, stabilizes, and protects mechanisms — ensuring they remain themselves across time, operators, and conditions.

Identity integrity is treated as a first-class property of any mechanism, not an incidental side effect.
